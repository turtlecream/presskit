Description

RP7 is a slot-managing roguelike. Instead of character control, you can roll seven random encounter slots to create your character's journey. Roll and re-roll each step of your journey as you plan ahead. With one simple mechanic, you'll experience infinite RPG gameplay. Fight monsters, gather golds, be more strong, and find your missing pet frog!

History

In 2017, RP7 started it's development journey as RP6, a tiny experimental prototype by Sun Park. While modifying the game as a full verion, the project moved to studio project of Turtle Cream which founded by Sun since 2009. Sun and Youngmin started to make this game as pixel graphics. And after trying some art experiments with various artist, Peter joined the team and make them all 3D art style.

Features

  • Strange but familiar RPG gameplay with 7 buttons
  • 7 playable character classes
  • 7 randomly assigned levels
  • And many other 7 things
